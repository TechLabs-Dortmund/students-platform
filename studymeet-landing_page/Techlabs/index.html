How it works

Your way to exam success!

01

Login

Create a personal account quickly, easily and chat directly with your fellow students!

02

Create a group

To create a group, go to 'My Groups' and use the button 'Create group'. All the relevant information can now be inserted and you only need to confirm it! Remember to set the access to public so that people can find your study group!

03

Join a group

Want it even simpler? Then simply join already existing groups and immediately benefit from a focused environment!

Group type!

Instead of randomly joining a group to see if you guys match or not, you can already decide beforehand what the purpose of the group is! Is it talking online, doing homework together or do you just want to meet people from your uni?

Meeting time!

You can select a meeting day for your study group so that no one forgets when the time comes to whine about how hard the lecture was this week! Just adjust the times according to your needs!

Location!

You can search for people at your university or look for people somewhere in Germany! Take advantage of a local community, or expand your horizons and connect with people from other regions!

Easy search!

Our interface is made to be simple and fast! You can easily filter the perfect study group with our filter functions! There is still a lot to express your creativity in the group description!
